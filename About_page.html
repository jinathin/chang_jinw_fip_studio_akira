About Studio AKIRA's team

Studio Akira started out in the Vancouver, Canada. We developed our portfolio shooting mostly travel, fashion and product photography for local's commercial sector. In 2018 Studio Akira launched in it's new headquarters in London. The list of specialities quickly grew to a very diverse list. Experts in storytelling, and masters of light, Kristy and Jin adapted to the needs of our clients and were able to make almost anything look beautiful and cinematic. With aspirations to one day reach the globe through short films, documentaries and photographs, the projects we work on today are all steps towards that dream. So let us introduce you to our team.
